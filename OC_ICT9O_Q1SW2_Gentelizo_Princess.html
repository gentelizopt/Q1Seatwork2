Characters of Noli Me Tangere

Image Name of the Character Description
Crisostomo Ibarra The main character of Noli me Tangere. He studied in Europe for 7 years and returned to the Philippines.
Maria Clara The daughter produced from an affair between Padre Damaso and Captain Tiago's Wife. She is known as Ibarra's love interest.
Padre Damaso A greedy, evil, abusive and corrupt Spanish priest. He is one of the main antagonists in the story.
Kapitan Tiago A rich Filipino man that has connections with many officials of the Catholic Church. He is also known as Maria Clara's "father".
Don Rafael Ibarra's father and one of the richest men in San Diego. He was called an erehe and pilibustero by the church.
